1. WELCOME TO TECHCORP

Welcome to TechCorp Industries! We're thrilled to have you join our team of innovators and problem-solvers. This handbook serves as your guide to understanding our company culture, policies, and procedures.

2. COMPANY MISSION AND VALUES

Mission: To transform businesses through innovative technology solutions that drive growth and efficiency.

Core Values:
- Innovation: We embrace creative thinking and continuous improvement
- Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency in all dealings
- Collaboration: We work together to achieve common goals
- Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do
- Customer Focus: We prioritize our customers' success

3. CODE OF CONDUCT

All employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues with respect and professionalism
- Maintain confidentiality of company and client information
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Report any violations of company policies
- Represent the company positively in all interactions

4. EMPLOYMENT POLICIES

At-Will Employment: Employment with TechCorp is at-will, meaning either party can terminate the relationship at any time.

Equal Opportunity: TechCorp is an equal opportunity employer and does not discriminate based on race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, or veteran status.

Background Checks: All offers of employment are contingent upon successful completion of background checks.

5. WORK HOURS AND ATTENDANCE

Standard Hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday
Flexible Work: Many positions offer flexible scheduling with manager approval
Remote Work: Eligible employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week

Attendance Requirements:
- Notify your manager at least 30 minutes before your start time if you'll be late or absent
- Excessive tardiness or absences may result in disciplinary action

6. LEAVE POLICIES

Paid Time Off (PTO):
- 0-2 years: 15 days
- 3-5 years: 20 days
- 6+ years: 25 days

Sick Leave: 10 days per year (separate from PTO)

Holidays: 12 company holidays plus 2 floating holidays

Parental Leave:
- Primary caregiver: 12 weeks paid
- Secondary caregiver: 6 weeks paid

Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family, 3 days for extended family

7. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

Performance Reviews: Conducted annually in December with mid-year check-ins

Goal Setting: Employees work with managers to set SMART goals quarterly

Career Development: Access to training programs, mentorship, and tuition reimbursement

8. COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS

Pay Schedule: Bi-weekly on Fridays via direct deposit

Benefits Overview:
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision)
- 401(k) with 6% company match
- Life insurance (2x annual salary)
- Short and long-term disability
- Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)
- Wellness programs
- Commuter benefits

9. HEALTH AND SAFETY

Workplace Safety:
- Report all accidents and injuries immediately
- Participate in safety training programs
- Follow all posted safety procedures

Emergency Procedures:
- Fire: Use nearest exit, meet at designated assembly point
- Medical: Call 911 and notify security
- Security threat: Follow lockdown procedures

Drug-Free Workplace: TechCorp maintains a drug-free workplace. Violation of this policy will result in immediate termination.

10. TECHNOLOGY AND SECURITY

Acceptable Use:
- Company equipment is for business use primarily
- Limited personal use is permitted if it doesn't interfere with work
- All activity on company devices may be monitored

Security Requirements:
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
- Lock screens when away from desk
- Report lost or stolen devices immediately
- Do not share login credentials
- Follow data classification and handling procedures

Social Media Policy:
- Do not share confidential information
- Include disclaimer when discussing work-related topics
- Be respectful and professional
